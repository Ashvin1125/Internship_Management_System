# Internship Management System - Experience Guide

Welcome to the **Internship Management System (IMS)**. This guide explains how to navigate the portal, manage your work, and use the professional features available to you.

## 1. Getting Started
The portal uses a secure, "Isolated Session" system.
- **Access**: When you visit the portal, you are automatically assigned a secure browsing context (look for the unique ID in your browser's address bar).
- **Multiple Accounts**: To log into two different accounts (e.g., as an Admin and a Student) at the same time, simply open the Login page in a new browser tab. Each tab operates independently.

---

## 2. Admin Portal (Supervision)
Admins oversee the entire internship lifecycle.
- **User Setup**: Create Guides and Students in seconds.
- **Pairing**: Connect students with appropriate faculty mentors through the **"Assign Guide"** tool.
- **Monitoring**: Deep-dive into student profiles, review their uploads, and maintain system integrity.

## 3. Guide Portal (Mentorship)
Guides provide direct technical and professional supervision.
- **Status Review**: Monitor daily logs submitted by students. You can **Approve** or **Reject** entries with personalized feedback.
- **Task Assignment**: Directly assign specific tasks with deadlines.
- **Document Assessment**: Access the "Documents" section for any assigned student to download and review their project PPTs, reports, or research papers.

## 4. Student Portal (Logbook)
The Student portal is your central command for internship tracking.
- **Daily Diary**: Log your daily activities with the "Zero Refresh" interface—your data is saved without reloading the entire page.
- **Task List**: Track the status of tasks assigned by your guide. Update them as "In Progress" or "Completed."
- **My Documents**: Securely upload and manage your project documentation. Supports DOC, PDF, ZIP, and presentation files.

---

## 5. Seamless Experience
- **Instant Updates**: Form submissions across the portal use "Zero Refresh" technology. Look for the professional success notifications that pop up after saving.
- **Mobile Friendly**: The interface is fully responsive, allowing you to manage your internship from a tablet or desktop.
- **Secure by Design**: Your data is protected by industry-standard security protocols designed to prevent unauthorized access.
